My thoughts tend to gravitate towards renewable energy, the ecosystem, philosphy, and javacript.

Energy

If there is a will there is a way. Energy has shifted dramatically over the last 10,000 years. With the introduction to fire humans began a revolution. No more waiting for energy.

However, our ways of producing energy have been come unsustainable. In a world with more energy than ever concieved we still forget about one last step.

The Waste.

What happens when this energy source is deplete or this tools use is obsolete. How can we take trash and turn it to gold. One particular example that lingers on my mind is the endless fleet of automobiles.

Ecosystem

Culture across the globe is spreading faster than wildfire. Fashion, food, and plants are all being imported and exported everywhere. This vast culturalization is altering our local ecosystems right under our noses.

Foreign species have advanced into our local food chains, disrupting a unqiue balance. Our ecosystems are being attacked at the very base wich supports them, the plants. Native plants have supported our environments for thousands of year have to power to reinvigorate these environments.

Our world is developing, whether or not humans are around. I want to enjoy it while I'm around.
